But what exactly is this?

Recursion. Sequence. Ring a bell?

They are all hard to grasp by reading the pages of a book or listening to a lecturer's words. Videos might help you to understand it, but not everybody has that kind of time. How do we know? Well, we were once there.

Once there? Are you guys no longer Computer Science students?

We are still computer science students. Yes, we were once there. We are no longer there.

We learnt that the best way to learn hard concpets is by coding them out and we tried it.

It worked. For us.

So, why not tell the whole world?

That is the reason you are here.

This project is our way of telling the world what we have learnt.

What does it contain?

Everything. That is the short answer.
